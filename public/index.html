FAQ

What is iZip?

Answer: Similar to Quizziz and Kahoot our website provides a fun, competitive learning environment. Designed to be simple yet engaging for students in grade 1 - 3.

How do I use iZip as a teacher?

Answer: Once you click the start button, select the teacher option, from there you can host and create your own quiz that can be answered by your students.

How do I use iZip as a student?

Answer: Once you click the start button, select the student option, from there input your teacher's code and your display name, press enter then have fun!

Does iZip offer any interactive activities?

Answer: Yes, iZip offers a variety of interactive activities that are designed to be engaging and pleasing to the eye of the students.

Can I play a quiz more than once?

Answer: Yes, you can take the quiz again as long as the host is willing to do it again.
